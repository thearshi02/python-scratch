#for loop-
Used when you know in advance how many times you want to run the block of the code. It typically iterates over a sequence or a range of a numbers.

#The range() function-
Generate a sequence of numbers.

#while loop-
Repeats a block of code as long as specific condition remains true. 

#loop control(break , continue and pass)-
break- immediately exits the loop entirely.
continue- skips the rest of the current iteration and jumps straight to the next turn of the loop.
